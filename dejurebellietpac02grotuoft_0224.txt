Of the first point3 Hanno inveighs against Annibal, that, not ad·

Nations. Which however is not to be understood so absolutely3 for
the Law of Nations does not prescribe that all ambassadors are to be
admitted3 but that they are not to be excluded without cause.

The cause may be on the part of him who sends, of him who is
sent, or in the fact that he is sent.
